Crafting Robust & Scalable Design Solutions For Brands And Humans.

Hello. My name is Swati and I work with smart and passionate folks at Appinventiv as Creative Head.

I believe in concept-driven, iterative design work and broadening my horizons. I tend to craft visual communication for brands, people, and discerning systems everywhere. I have ten years of extensive experience in product design, strategy and leadership for cross functional and creative teams.

Recently I have been fully engaged with corporate branding for Appinventiv and leading business analysis and visual communication for VEME- the world's first creative platform build on blockchain framework for short form viral videos, that exist to liberate and reward everyone's creative expression.

My design journey

I started my career as web designer for a small time IT company based in Noida. Because I wanted to be closer to the product and work in collaboration with product owners and managers, I shifted to a tech start-up called AppStudioz as Sr. Creative Designer. There I build the design team of 30+ creative professionals from grounds up.

Since last three year, I am happily crafting successful product experiences for Appinventiv, one of India's leading mobile app development company. To know more about my professional journey, download my resume.
